LinguaFlix

Show secondary subtitles in your language when you pause Netflix. Learn languages while watching your favorite shows.

Add to Chrome — It's Free

How It Works

1

Pause

Hit pause on any Netflix show or movie while subtitles are on screen.

2

Read

A second subtitle in your chosen language appears at the top of the screen.

3

Play

Press play and the overlay disappears. Keep watching, keep learning.

Features

⚡

Instant Overlay

Subtitles appear the moment you pause — no delay, no extra clicks. Uses Netflix's own subtitle data for accuracy.

▢

Fullscreen Support

Works seamlessly in fullscreen mode. The overlay follows you wherever you watch.

🌐

11 Languages

Choose from English, Polish, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Turkish, Swedish, and Norwegian.

🔒

Privacy First

No data collection. No tracking. No accounts. Everything runs locally in your browser.
